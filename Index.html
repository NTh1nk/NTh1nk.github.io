ProjectEuler
What is project euler? Project euler is a curated list of mathematics and programming problems, they can be solved with almost any programming language, i will be solving them with a mix of p5 and vanilla JS Project Euler is a series of challenging mathematical/computer programming problems that will require more than just mathematical insights to solve. Although mathematics will help you arrive at elegant and efficient methods, the use of a computer and programming skills will be required to solve most problems. The motivation for starting Project Euler, and its continuation, is to encourage the development of important problem- solving skills and techniques, whilst providing a platform for the ingenuity of programmers rather than development of programming skills.
