Behind the meme

  • What is Rickrolling? Rickrolling is a prank and an internet meme involving the music video for the 1987 Rick Astley song "Never Gonna Give You Up". The meme is typically a bait and switch: a user clicks on a link expecting something interesting to happen, only to be led to the Astley video.
  • Origin It originated from duckrolling, a practice common on the site called "4Chan" in the early 2000, this practice included sending a link saying that it was something great/new, for example a new video game, and then when the person clicked the link, it would be a picture of a duck instead.
  • The first Rickroll Just picture this: GTA 4 is releasing any moment now, so everyone floods to the GTA website(Rocktstar), to watch the trailer, but its DOWN, so what do you do.
  • When did it become popular Rickrolling became popular around 2005-2008
